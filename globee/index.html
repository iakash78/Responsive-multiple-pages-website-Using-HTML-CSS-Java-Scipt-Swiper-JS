Frequently Asked Questions

Can I create a website without knowing how to code?

Absolutely. GLOBEe is for everyone and makes it possible to create a high-quality website without knowing how to code. In the GLOBEe, you can drag and drop any feature you want and customize it to match the look and feel of your site.

Can I purchase a domain through Google?

Google can help you purchase a domain through one of our domain host partners. During sign up, simply select the option to 'Buy a new domain'. We'll then guide you through the process to help you set up Google Workspace for your new domain.

Can I still use the previous version of Sites?

If your company is using any previous version of Sites, there will be no disruption. Keep editing and sharing your Sites as you have previously.

Can external visitors access a company site?

Yes. People outside your company can access your site, even without a Google Workspace account. You can also opt to restrict access through sharing settings.

Can I host my website with GLOBEe?

Sure! As soon as your website is ready, just pick the name and publish the website to *.globee.com just like that. You will be able to share it with your customers.

Will my website be Google-friendly?

No doubt that GLOBEe functions properly, as it is created with Google-supported technologies. Along with that, the website builder offers multiple themes that utilize Google Accelerated Mobile Pages - making your websites lightning fast.
